More about me

I was born in Kiev, Ukraine, in 1987 - not a whole lot of time before the Soviet Union fell apart.
My family made Aliyah when I was just 3 years old, and we lived in Tel Aviv for about two years.
Then, we moved to Yavne (near Rehovot and Ashdod), where I grew up.
After my army service I moved to Bat Yam, where I worked with at risk youth as head of the local music division at an NGO called "Elem".
After Bat-Yam, I lived in Yafo (near the beach!) for just a bit more than a year, and them finally came back to Tel Aviv.
I left Tel aviv for Givatayim just a month ago, but I don't know if that actually counts as moving to a different city :)
