Welcome to "Not the BBC"

I am a historian with an interest in the weird religions of the nineteenth-century. I am currently writing a book about Helena Blavatsky's work, The Voice of the Silence

Helena Blavatsky was the founder of the Theosophical Society and the "great-grandmother" of the New Age Movement. I wrote my PhD on her.

My 2019 book, Recycled Lives was a historical study of Blavatsky's theory of reincarnation. This theory is more influential today than many people realise.

This volume of essays, which I edited with Boaz Huss, came out last month. It is the first volume dedicated to the mysterious "Cosmic Movement."
